About Me

Hi, I'm Lorenzo Ortiz, an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) currently residing in Yakima, WA. I play a crucial role in providing timely and quality care to patients in various emergency situations. My experience in electronic medical records enhances the efficiency and accuracy of emergency response and documentation.
